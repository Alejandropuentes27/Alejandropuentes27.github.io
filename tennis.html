Facts About Tennis

  • Tennis increases your life span by 10 years
  • The fastest recorded tennis shot is 163 mph
  • The word tennis comes from the word 'tenez' in French
  • On average, a player runs around 3 miles per match
  • Tennis balls used to be white but then changed to yellow so it was easier to see on TV
  • The sport dates back to 12th century France
